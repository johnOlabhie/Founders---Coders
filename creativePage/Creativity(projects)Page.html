This collaborative effort underscores my ability to contribute effectively within a group dynamic.

  • Vitural Reality project(Unity)/Bender 3d

    1. Independently, I delve into the realm of 3D modeling using Blender. This creative outlet allows me to bring virtual concepts to life, infusing a sense of realism into my projects. These meticulously crafted 3D models find their purpose in my ongoing Unity VR application project, "Mind Space." This innovative endeavor aims to create a virtual fine motor skills development app, leveraging the immersive capabilities of VR technology to provide users with a unique and engaging experience.

  • Digital Art and Design:

    1. Embarking on a creative odyssey, I have ventured into the realm of digital book writing with a project titled "Celestial Legends." This literary endeavor transcends conventional storytelling, as I weave tales inspired by celestial Greek and Egyptian mythology. Adding a unique twist, I infuse the narratives with a teenage perspective, exploring the vast possibilities of infinite universes.

    2. Each page of "Celestial Legends" unfolds not only through words but also through self-painted artwork. These self-painted illustrations serve as visual companions, enriching the storytelling experience and offering a glimpse into the vivid landscapes of celestial mythology.
